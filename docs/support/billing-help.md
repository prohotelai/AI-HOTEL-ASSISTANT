## Billing FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How does billing work?**
A: Subscriptions are billed monthly on the day you subscribe. For example, if you subscribe on January 15th, your next billing date will be February 15th.

**Q: Can I change my plan at any time?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept:
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- ACH bank transfers (US only)
- Wire transfers (Enterprise plans only)
- PayPal (selected regions)

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start the trial. You'll be billed after the trial ends unless you cancel.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

### Usage & Limits

**Q: What happens if I exceed my usage limits?**
A: 
- **AI Messages:** Service pauses until next billing cycle or you upgrade
- **Voice Minutes:** Voice features disabled until reset/upgrade
- **Support Tickets:** Cannot create new tickets until reset/upgrade
- **Storage:** Cannot upload new documents until you free up space or upgrade

**Q: How is usage calculated?**
- **AI Messages:** Each user message to AI Assistant counts as 1 message
- **Voice Minutes:** Rounded up to nearest minute per call
- **Support Tickets:** Each created ticket counts toward monthly limit
- **Storage:** Total size of uploaded documents and attachments

**Q: Do unused messages/minutes roll over?**
A: No, usage limits reset on your billing date each month. Unused quota does not carry over.

**Q: Can I purchase additional usage?**
A: Not currently. Please upgrade to a higher plan if you regularly exceed limits.

### Upgrading & Downgrading

**Q: How do I upgrade my plan?**
A:
1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing**
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select higher plan
4. Confirm payment
5. Upgrade takes effect immediately

**Q: Will I be charged immediately when upgrading?**
A: You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing period. For example, if you upgrade halfway through your billing month, you'll pay 50% of the price difference.

**Q: How do downgrades work?**
A: Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll continue to have access to your current plan features until then.

**Q: What happens to my data when downgrading?**
A: Your data is preserved, but features may be limited:
- Excess storage files remain (read-only) until you delete or upgrade
- Historical messages/tickets are archived but not deleted
- Integrations may be disabled based on new plan limits

### Payment Issues

**Q: My payment failed. What should I do?**
A:
1. Verify card details are correct
2. Check card has sufficient funds
3. Contact your bank (may be fraud protection)
4. Update payment method in Dashboard > Billing
5. Retry payment manually
6. Contact support if issue persists

**Q: Why was I charged twice?**
A: This is rare but can happen if:
- You upgraded mid-cycle (prorated charge + regular charge)
- Your bank pre-authorized the payment (temporary hold, not actual charge)
- System error (contact support immediately for refund)

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
A:
1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing > Payment Methods**
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. Enter new card details
4. Set as default
5. Remove old method if desired

**Q: Can I get an invoice/receipt?**
A: Yes! Invoices are automatically emailed after each payment. You can also download them:
1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing > Invoices**
2. Click invoice date
3. Download PDF

### Refunds & Cancellations

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Refunds are provided on a case-by-case basis:
- **Free trial:** No charge, no refund needed
- **First month:** Full refund if unsatisfied (within 30 days)
- **Subsequent months:** Prorated refunds for extenuating circumstances
- **Annual plans:** Refund for unused months (after first month)

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A:
1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing**
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select reason (optional feedback)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Account remains active until billing period ends

**Q: What happens after I cancel?**
- Your account remains active until the end of current billing period
- After expiration, you'll be downgraded to FREE (STARTER) plan
- Data is preserved for 90 days (then archived)
- You can reactivate anytime within 90 days

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Not currently. You must cancel and resubscribe when ready. Contact support for special circumstances (e.g., seasonal hotels).

- **Jan 1-31:** Continue with paid features

## Troubleshooting Payment Issues

### Payment Declined
1. **Check card expiration:** Update if expired
2. **Verify billing address:** Must match card details
3. **Check funds/credit limit:** Ensure sufficient balance
4. **Contact bank:** May be fraud protection
5. **Try different card:** Use backup payment method
6. **Contact support:** If all else fails

### Unexpected Charges
1. **Review invoice details:** Dashboard > Billing > Invoices
2. **Check for plan changes:** May have upgraded
3. **Look for prorated charges:** Common with mid-cycle changes
4. **Verify no duplicate subscriptions:** Check email confirmations
5. **Contact billing support:** For unrecognized charges

### Subscription Not Active After Payment
1. **Wait 10-15 minutes:** Webhook processing delay
2. **Check email:** Look for payment confirmation
3. **Refresh dashboard:** Log out and back in
4. **Clear browser cache:** May show old subscription status
5. **Contact support:** If not resolved within 1 hour
